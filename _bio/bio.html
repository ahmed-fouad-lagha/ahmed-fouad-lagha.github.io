I am a PhD Candidate at Eötvös Loránd University (ELTE) specializing in **Synthetic Data Generation** and **Privacy Preservation**. My research interests lie at the intersection of Machine Learning, Privacy, and Security, with a particular focus on Differential Privacy and Generative Models.

I am passionate about building secure and private machine learning systems that can benefit society while protecting individual privacy.
